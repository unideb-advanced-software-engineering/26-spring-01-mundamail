# Data Sovereignity

## What does it mean?
<https://quality.arc42.org/qualities/data-sovereignty>

## Why is it important?
Data processed by the system must be stored within Zamunda's territory, and the system must be operated within Zamunda's territory.
Users' emails must be stored in encrypted form.

# Latency

## What does it mean?
<https://quality.arc42.org/qualities/latency>

## Why is it important?
The internet coverage will not be perfect in certain areas of the country as there may be higher delays and lower bandwidths even where there is internet connection
